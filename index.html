Installing

The modpack can be installed in any Modrinth-supporting launcher, but I recommend using ATLauncher as it can both update private Modrinth packs, and manage your Java installation - so it's less work for you!

Steps

  1. Download the ATLauncher
  2. Login with your Minecraft account
  3. Navigate to the Packs tab
  4. Click on "Modrinth" on the left-hand side
  5. Click on "Add Manually" and enter the pack's ID XXIblqAD
  6. Your pack should now be installed under the Instances tab!
  7. Read the post-installation steps below!!!

Post-installation steps:

  • It's recommended to increase the amount of RAM allocated to the pack to ~6GiB, which you can do by opening the instance's settings and changing the Maximum Memory value to 6144. Don't do anything higher than 10GiB
  • If you want to get relatively consistent performance out of your game, consider adding these JVM flags in your instance's settings:

Help

Improving performance:

There are a lot of mods in this pack, which may take a toll on your hardware. If you're struggling to get decent frames out of the game, try the following:

  • Reduce your render distance
  • If you're lagging during rain, disable features of Psection Rain in the mod menu
  • If you're having very specific issues (mods crashing etc.), reach out to me directly
